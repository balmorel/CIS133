PTR Website Critique


Three design principles that have been well-implemented:

  • Navigation and header areas are consistent throughout the website creating a degree
    of familiarity that is consistent with best practices.

  • Use of color is attractive and consistent with varying degrees of blues and white that are reminiscent
    of the ocean and sky; it suits the business's niche.

  • The creator of the website has adhered to elements of writing for the web. There is
    consistent use of headers and subheaders and the paragraphs have been kept brief to
    account for contemporary attention spans.

Three design practices that could be implemented in a better way:

  • Content has a number of spelling errors ("streucturees" on the "yurts" page) that detract from
    the integrity of the website and therefore the business.

  • The designers could have done a better job with the various header images and their image quality.
    It looks like they have lost the aspect ratio of the images - they appear to have been stretche wide.

  • Use of white space and the relationship with the text is not well-balanced (particularly on
    the homepage). There is a disproportionate amount of whitespace to the right of the page
    which creates an awkward eye pull away from important content.

Other ways I would I improve this website:

Greater use of media and images:
This website should have, at the very least, images of what a Yurt dwelling entails.
How about some video of guests enjoying the kayaking experience or bird watching?
Potential customers want to have some visual aided feel of the activities and accomodations.

Accentuating the Dining Experience:
When someone is pondering a vacation like this one, dining is traditionally one of the most
important elements of the overall experience. The designers have not included any content
featuring the bounty of food that awaits them should they choose Pacific Trails Resort.

Greater Personalization:
It would be nice to feel a personal touch with images of the staff interacting with guests, or
an 'about us' section that introduces the various staff and their repsonsibilities.
